Digital magazine proposal

Irene Lin

Introduction

DigiArt

A magazine highlighting different digital artists with different styles, showcasing their journey and how they developed their art to what it has become today. It will also feature tips and tricks on how to open your own online store, dos and don’ts, and more helpful information. Digital art aims to bring exposure to artists and the expansive styles unique to them.
