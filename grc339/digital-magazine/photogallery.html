DTIYS for the month of July

Some draw this in your style challenges new this month! DTIYS is an art challenge, where artists draw an already existing artwork, but in their own style.

There are no restrictions regarding tools, you can use both: digital and traditional media, and there’s also no time limit. Also, if you have your own ideas, want to draw the character in a different pose, change colors, you are free to do so. Usually, when an artist decides to host the “draw this in your art style” challenge, he/she posts an artwork with the hashtag and explanation including rules (what is allowed and what’s not).
